Exercises

Dumbbell Shoulder Press

Benefits:

  • Builds Shoulder Strength and Stability
  • Enhances Muscle Symmetry
  • Improves Overhead Stability
  • Strengthens Core Stability

Instructions:

1. Setup: Hold dumbbells at shoulder height, palms forward, with a stable stance or seated on a bench.

2. Press: Push dumbbells upward, extending arms fully without locking elbows, keeping them slightly in front of your head.

3. Lower: Slowly return to the starting position, maintaining control.

4.Tips: Keep your core engaged, back straight, and shoulders relaxed. Avoid using momentum or shrugging.

Barbell Lunge

Benefits:

  • Builds Lower Body Strength
  • Improves Balance and Stability
  • Enhances Functional Mobility
  • Boosts Athletic Performance

Instructions:

1. Setup: Place the barbell on your upper back, keeping your feet shoulder-width apart.

2. Step Forward: Take a big step forward with one leg, lowering your hips to form a 90-degree angle in both knees.

3. Push Back: Push through the front heel to return to the starting position.

4.Tip: Keep your chest lifted, knees behind your toes, and engage your core for stability.

Battle Rope Wave

Benefits:

  • Builds Full-Body Strength
  • Improves Cardiovascular Fitness
  • Boosts Coordination and Stability
  • Burns Calories

Instructions:

1. Setup: Hold the ends of the rope in both hands with feet shoulder-width apart, knees slightly bent, and core engaged.

2. Create Waves: Rapidly alternate raising and lowering each arm to create continuous ripples in the rope.

3. Maintain Form:Keep your back straight, chest lifted, and use controlled movements.

4.Tips: Focus on consistent wave patterns and proper breathing to maximize effectiveness.

Lat pulldown

Benefits:

  • Strengthens the Back
  • Enhances Pulling Strength
  • Improves Shoulder Stability
  • Aesthetic Development

Instructions:

1. Setup: Sit on the pulldown machine with feet flat on the floor and adjust the thigh pad to secure your legs.

2. Grip the Bar: Use a wide overhand grip slightly wider than shoulder-width.

3. Pull Down:Lean slightly back, engage your core, and pull the bar toward your chest and squeeze your shoulder blades together at the bottom of the movement.

4.Tips: Avoid using momentum; focus on controlled movements and keep your shoulders down and avoid shrugging.

Biking

Benefits:

  • Cardiovascular Health
  • Strengthens Lower Body
  • Mental Well-being
  • Weight Management

Instructions:

1. Setup:Adjust seat height for a slight knee bend at the pedal's lowest point and ensure handlebars are comfortable and aligned.

2.Start Slowly: Begin at an easy pace for warm-up and gradually increase speed or resistance.

3.Maintain Proper Form:Keep your back straight and shoulders relaxed and grip handlebars lightly.

4.Safety Tips: Wear a helmet and reflective gear and use bike lights when cycling in low visibility.

Incline Walk/Run

Benefits:

  • Improved Cardiovascular Health
  • Enhanced Calorie Burn
  • Strengthened Lower Body Muscles
  • Increased Endurance:

Instructions:

1. Setup: Adjust the treadmill incline to a comfortable level (start with 3-5% and gradually increase)and ensure proper footwear for grip and cushioning.

2. Posture: Keep your back straight, shoulders relaxed, and look forward (not at your feet) and avoid holding onto the treadmill handrails.

3. Cool Down:Gradually decrease the incline and speed for 5–10 minutes to allow your heart rate to return to normal.

4.Tips:Stay hydrated during and after the workout and monitor your heart rate if using a treadmill with sensors to stay within your target zone.

kick boxing

Benefits:

  • Improves Cardiovascular Health
  • Enhances Strength and Power
  • Burns Calories and Supports Weight Loss
  • mproves Mental Health

Instructions:

1. Warm-Up: Begin with dynamic stretches, jogging, or jumping jacks for 5-10 minutes.

2. Punches: Practice jabs, crosses, hooks, and uppercuts with controlled motion.

3. Kicks:Incorporate front kicks, roundhouse kicks, and sidekicks with proper hip rotation.

4.Tips: Engage your core for balance and power, keep movements controlled to avoid injury, and focus on technique before speed.

Shoulder Press

Benefits:

  • Builds Shoulder Strength
  • Improves Overhead Stability
  • Strengthens Core
  • Enhances Mobility and Grip Strength

Instructions:

1. Setup: Hold kettlebells at shoulder height with palms facing inward, elbows close to your torso and stand with feet shoulder-width apart or sit on a bench for stability.

2. Press: Push the kettlebells upward in a controlled motion, keeping them slightly in front of your head.

3.Lower:Slowly bring the kettlebells back down to the starting position, keeping control throughout.

4.Tips:Engage your core and avoid arching your lower back and move in a straight, smooth path to avoid unnecessary strain on shoulders.

Roller Skating

Benefits:

  • Cardiovascular Health
  • Strengthens Muscles
  • Low-Impact Workout
  • Stress Relief

Instructions:

1. Start Position:Stand with knees slightly bent and arms relaxed for balance.

2. Basic Movement:Push off with one foot while gliding with the other and alternate feet in a skating motion while keeping your body balanced.

3. Stopping:Use the toe stop on your skates to slow down or stop.

4.Tips: Start slow and practice balance before increasing speed and avoid crowded areas and check for obstacles in your path.

Yoga

Benefits:

  • Improves Flexibility
  • Reduces Stress
  • Boosts Breathing
  • Strengthens Muscles

Instructions:

1. Set Your Space: Choose a quiet and comfortable area.

2.Focus on Breathing: Inhale deeply and exhale slowly during each pose.

3. Stay Present:Focus on your body and breath to stay in the moment.

4.Cool Down:Finish with relaxation poses like Child’s Pose or Savasana.
